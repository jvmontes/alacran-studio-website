Jorge Viramontes
Founder, CEO

Jorge Viramontes is the founder of Alacrán Studio. He founded Alacrán Studio in 2020 after seeing the need for his community to have access to create excellent digital experiences.

His mission is to support small business owners and to grow the economy in community, supporting one-another and building partnership based on authenticity.

Jorge graduated from The University of Michigan in 2013 and started his career as a Technical Consultant at Solstice Mobile, working with Fortune 500 companies to build software products. He grew up in Chicago, and loves playing his acoustic guitar and going for walks at the park in his free time.
